BAGIS symposium 2025​

We are happy to announce that the BAGIS Symposium 2025 will be held at Chiba University, Japan, on March 21, 2025. The BAGIS (Formation of a Strategic Base in Asia Creating and Developing Global Minded Imaging Science) is a project supported by the JSPS “Research Center Formation Project-B. Formation of Academic Foundations for Asia and Africa (Grant No: JPJSCCB20220006)”. The project aims to develop a research center for imaging science and technology connecting Japan and Southeast Asia. Chiba University serves as the core research institution (Japanese collaborators: University of Tsukuba, Tokyo Polytechnic University, and Oita National College of Technology) and conducts research exchanges with universities in seven Southeast Asian countries (Thailand, Turkey, Malaysia, Vietnam, Cambodia, the Philippines, and Indonesia). We aimed to promote joint research, human resource development, and exchange through international conferences and symposiums. The BAGIS program will end this school year (March), and we will hold a symposium to wrap up the program. The BAGIS symposium will consist of keynote and invited lectures. We also invite general presentations from Asian countries, mainly from BAGIS participating members. We invite in-person and online presentations on various topics related to imaging sciences.
